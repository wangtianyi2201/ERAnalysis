Developed an end-to-end pipeline for high-frequency trading (HFT) that integrates data engineering and reinforcement learning. Collected and processed real-time order book data from Binance, applied clustering techniques to identify individual market-making bots and classify their strategies. Leveraged game theory to model multi-agent interactions and compute Nash equilibrium, enabling strategic reasoning about opponents. Trained a DDPG agent in an online RL environment that continuously updates based on real-time market conditions and opponent strategies. Introduced a novel reward mechanism based on Nash payoffs. The system demonstrated superior performance over traditional market making baselines in terms of profitability and Sharpe ratio.
